Poha 🍚

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 25 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 medium potato, diced (optional)
  • 1/4 cup peas
  • 1/4 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 8–10 curry leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Recipe:

1. Rinse poha in a strainer

2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.

3. Add curry leaves, chopped onions, green chilies, and sauté until onions are soft.

4. Add diced potatoes and peas. Cook until soft.

5. Sprinkle turmeric powder and salt, then add the soaked poha. Mix gently.

6. Cook for 2–3 minutes, then garnish with fresh coriander and a squeeze of lemon juice.

Paneer Masala 🍛

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Total Time: 35 mins

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2–3 tablespoons oil or butter
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream or milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Recipe:

1. Heat oil/butter in a pan and add cumin seeds.

2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.

4. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until oil separates.

5. Add paneer cubes and mix gently.

6. Stir in cream/milk and garam masala. Cook for 2–3 minutes.

7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.

Fried Rice 🍚

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 30 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe:

1. In a pan, scramble the eggs and set aside.

2. In the same pan, stir-fry mixed vegetables until tender.

3. Add cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing well.

4. Stir in scrambled eggs and green onions.

5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.

Lemon Rice 🍚

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 25 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (cooled)
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1–2 green chilies, chopped
  • 6–8 curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon roasted peanuts or cashews
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Recipe:

1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.

2. Add green chilies, curry leaves, and peanuts/cashews. Sauté for 1–2 minutes.

3. Add turmeric powder and salt, mix well.

4. Add the cooked rice and mix gently so the turmeric coats the rice evenly.

5. Turn off the heat and add lemon juice. Mix well.

6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
